Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Sigure Pte. Ltd. ("we," "us," "our") operates Prime Crush ("Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Account Information: Google account ID or Apple account ID for account identification
  • Game Data: Your progress, weekly scores, achievements, selected character, nickname
  • Device Information: OS, device model, language settings
  • Usage Data: Play time, gameplay actions, analytics
  • Technical Data: IP address, crash reports, error logs
  • Purchase Data: Transaction records for in-app purchases
  • Advertising Identifier: IDFA (iOS) or Google Advertising ID (Android), used for ad delivery and measurement. You can reset or limit this in your device settings.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Ad impressions, clicks, and viewability metrics
  • Approximate Location: Derived from IP address for ad geo-targeting. We do not collect precise GPS location.

We do NOT collect:

  • Real name, email, phone number, or address (managed by Google/Apple)
  • Payment information (processed by Google Play or Apple App Store)
  • Precise device location (GPS)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Save and sync your game progress
  • Improve the Game and fix bugs
  • Process in-app purchases and deliver purchased items
  • Prevent cheating and abuse
  • Provide customer support
  • Analyze game usage
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob to support free access to the Game. Where consent is provided, ads may be personalized; otherwise we serve non-personalized ads.

3. Data Storage

  • Your data is stored on Firebase (Google) servers
  • We use encryption and security measures to protect your data
  • We cannot guarantee absolute security

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your information. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google): For data storage and analytics
  • Google Play (Google): For account and payment management on Android
  • Apple: For account and payment management on iOS
  • Google AdMob and its mediation partners: For ad delivery, frequency capping, and measurement
  • Legal authorities: If required by law

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Object to processing
  • Delete your account directly within the Game settings

Contact us at contact@sigureltd.com to exercise these rights.

6. Advertising and Tracking Choices

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. To control how ads are personalized to you:

  • iOS: When you first open the Game, you will be asked to allow or deny app tracking via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT). You can change this anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Android: You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in Settings → Google → Ads.
  • European Economic Area / UK / Switzerland: We obtain your consent for personalized advertising via a consent screen presented at first launch (Google's User Messaging Platform). You can withdraw or change consent anytime through the Game's settings.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): California residents may opt out of the "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Contact contact@sigureltd.com to request this.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps, see https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 24 months
  • Deleted accounts: Data deleted within 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. We have configured Google AdMob to deliver non-child-directed ads only. If you believe a child under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA) is using the Game, please contact us so we can delete the data.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be stored on servers outside your country. We ensure appropriate protections for such transfers.
